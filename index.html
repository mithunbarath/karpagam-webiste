About Karpagam College

Located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, Karpagam College is known for its academic excellence, industry collaborations, and vibrant campus life.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to ignite curiosity and nurture leaders who drive societal transformation. Our mission focuses on quality education, impactful research, and holistic development.

  • Outcome-based curriculum
  • Industry-integrated learning
  • Research and innovation ecosystem
  • Inclusive and student-centric approach

Admissions

Join a vibrant community of learners. Admissions are based on merit and relevant entrance examinations as per Tamil Nadu guidelines.

How to Apply

  1. Submit the online application
  2. Upload required documents
  3. Appear for counseling/entrance as applicable
  4. Confirm admission and pay fees

Important Dates

  • Applications open: April 1
  • UG counseling: June – July
  • PG admissions: July – August
